## First Five Hires (Year 1)

Ordered by priority. Timing assumes advisory revenue funds early hires; co-development or seed capital funds the rest.

### Hire 1: VP of Energy Execution (Month 3-6)

**Role:** Leads interconnection strategy, BTM generation design, OEM relationships, equipment procurement. The technical backbone of the platform.

**Profile:**
- 10+ years in power project development, IPP operations, or utility engineering
- ERCOT interconnection experience (queue management, load studies, utility coordination)
- OEM relationships across gas engines, aero turbines, BESS
- Can lead site energy execution from concept through commissioning

**Comp range:** $200-300K base + equity (1-3% founding pool)

**Why first:** Energy execution is the core differentiator. Every advisory mandate, co-development deal, and campus project requires this capability. Without it, Cantus is a strategy firm, not a platform.

### Hire 2: Director of Business Development (Month 4-8)

**Role:** Tenant origination, EDC relationship management, deal flow pipeline. Supports Dom on origination while Dom focuses on co-development partnerships and capital relationships.

**Profile:**
- 7+ years in industrial/data center brokerage or corporate site selection
- Existing relationships with economic development organizations
- Tenant advisory experience (hyperscale, advanced manufacturing, or both)
- Texas market knowledge preferred

**Comp range:** $150-250K base + equity (0.5-1.5%)

### Hire 3: Chief of Staff / Head of Operations (Month 6-9)

**Role:** Platform operations, fund administration support, financial modeling, investor materials, board prep. The person who makes everything else run.

**Profile:**
- 5-8 years in infrastructure PE, real estate private equity, or development operations
- Financial modeling (project finance, fund economics, waterfall structures)
- Investor relations experience
- Can operate independently with minimal oversight

**Comp range:** $150-225K base + equity (0.5-1.5%)

### Hire 4: Community & Workforce Partnerships Lead (Month 9-12)

**Role:** EDC engagement, workforce pipeline development, community college/trade school partnerships, community impact framework. Per [[Strategy/Primary-Inputs-03-Labor-Community]], this is a foundational capability.

**Profile:**
- 7+ years in economic development, workforce development, or corporate community relations
- Existing EDC relationships in Texas
- Experience with WIOA, workforce commission programs, institutional partnerships
- Comfort in political/regulatory environment

**Comp range:** $120-180K base + equity (0.25-1%)

### Hire 5: Data & Intelligence Analyst (Month 9-12)

**Role:** CII product development. Parcel registry maintenance, site scoring models, ERCOT queue analysis, market intelligence production.

**Profile:**
- 3-5 years in geospatial analysis, real estate analytics, or energy market analysis
- GIS, data engineering, and visualization skills
- Familiarity with ERCOT data, utility planning documents, CoStar/similar platforms
- Can build and maintain the analytical infrastructure CII requires

**Comp range:** $100-150K base + equity (0.25-0.75%)

## Investor Framing

When presenting team to investors, emphasize:

1. **Founder-market fit:** Dom's unique position at the intersection of industrial real estate, powered infrastructure, and the AI/manufacturing convergence. No one else has built this exact capability set.

2. **Capability, not headcount:** The team is deliberately small at launch because the co-development model scales without headcount. Cantus leverages partner land, partner capital, and integrated intelligence — not bodies.

3. **Hire plan tied to revenue:** Each hire is funded by a specific revenue stream (advisory → energy VP; co-development → BD director; fund formation → chief of staff). No burn without revenue support.

4. **Advisory board as force multiplier:** Domain experts providing deal flow, credibility, and connections without payroll burden.
