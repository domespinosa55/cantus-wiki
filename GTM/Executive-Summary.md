# Cantus — Executive Summary

## The Opportunity

The United States is building two categories of factory simultaneously: AI factories (data centers) and manufacturing factories (semiconductors, batteries, defense, critical minerals). Both require the same physical inputs — reliable power at extraordinary scale and industrial-grade real estate — and both are constrained by the same bottleneck: there are not enough developers who can deliver powered industrial sites at the speed the market demands.

U.S. data center capacity must grow from 30 GW to 90-110 GW by 2030. Over $3 trillion in manufacturing reshoring has been announced. ERCOT alone is tracking 410 GW of large load interconnection requests against 103 GW of existing grid capacity. The infrastructure gap is measured in years and tens of billions of dollars.

## The Company

**Cantus is an integrated powered infrastructure platform** that develops, energizes, and operates industrial campuses for AI and advanced manufacturing tenants.

We are the developer who solves the execution bottleneck — aggregating infrastructure-dense land, manufacturing power as a deliverable output, and building campuses purpose-designed for the next generation of American industry.

## What Makes Cantus Different

**Vertical integration across four tiers:**

- **Land Aggregation:** Systematic origination of infrastructure-dense sites — embedded interconnection, substation adjacency, water rights — through direct acquisition and co-development partnerships with leading landowners.
- **Energy Execution:** Power as a developer-manufactured output. Behind-the-meter bridge generation delivering firm power in 12-18 months while utility interconnection completes on 36-48 month timelines. Equipment procurement relationships compressing lead times.
- **Tenant Platform:** Multi-tenant powered campuses co-locating AI and manufacturing tenants. Hyperscale anchor demand at $80-110/MWh enables advanced manufacturing tenancy at $50-65/MWh — globally competitive energy economics no single-tenant project can match.
- **Operated Capacity:** Progression from on-premises AI deployment (Cantus Edge — within the Technology Integration foundation) through distributed GPU hosting (Cantus Compute) to the customer-agnostic infrastructure prime contractor archetype (Cantus Prime), integrating across power-heavy industrial demand.

**No existing developer integrates all four tiers across both customer verticals.** Data center developers lack manufacturing capability. Industrial developers lack energy execution. IPPs don't build real estate. Cantus operates the full stack.
